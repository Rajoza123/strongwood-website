Our Story

Strength in build. Care in delivery.

STRONGWOOD Packaging Industries was founded in Ahmedabad with a single goal: to make wooden packaging that survives rough routes and still looks composed on arrival. We combine kiln-dried timber, smart fasteners, and moisture-safe finishes with rigorous QC checks.

Whether it's an urgent air freight crate or a seaworthy export box, we map your route, load, and handling conditions to engineer the right protection.
